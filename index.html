About Us

From a young age, Tammy developed a passion for food and has since made it a huge part of her life. Tammy loves to explore new flavors and techniques in the kitchen, and she is always excited to share her creations with others.

Tammy's love for food goes beyond just creating delicious dishes. She believes that food has the power to bring people together and make a positive impact in the world, and so is always looking for ways to give back to the community, whether by donating her time and energy to food banks or supporting conservation groups.

Tammy is highly attentive to her clients' dietary wants and needs. She understands that everyone has unique dietary requirements, and is dedicated to creating dishes that not only taste amazing but also cater to those specific needs. So whether you're celiac, dairy-free, plant-based, paleo, ayurvedic or omnivore, she is always willing to go above and beyond to ensure that you are satisfied with the food she serves.
